Our Story

More Than a
Code Factory

Brandon and Ashley Trebitowski started Treb Studios in Corrales, New Mexico in late 2012. What began as a two-person shop quickly grew as project after project exceeded expectations and a team of dedicated engineers came together.

In 2015, the company became Pixegon — a name that reflects a bigger mission: helping corporations, startups, and visionaries build software that truly matters. For over 15 years, that's exactly what we've done.

Unwavering dedication — We don't ship half-measures. Every project gets our full attention, start to finish.
Compassionate collaboration — We listen first, then build. Your problem becomes our problem.
Innovative spirit — We pick the right tools for the job, not the trending ones.
